Protection that acts in seconds: immediate response when it matters most

Got questions? Here are the answers.

About Using Teiker

Is it hard to use for older people?

Not at all. They just need to talk naturally. No buttons, no screens, no configuration. If they can hold a conversation, they can use Teiker.

Does it work even if someone isn't tech-savvy?

Yes. That's exactly what it's designed for. Our team installs it, sets it all up and leaves it running. All you need to do is talk.

Do you have to keep talking to Teiker all day?

No. Teiker is there when you need it. You can chat as much or as little as you like. Protection and monitoring continue either way.

About Privacy

Is Teiker recording everything I say?

No. Teiker does not record conversations. It only analyses what is necessary to help and protect you. Nothing is stored without your permission.

Who can see my information?

Only you and the family members you authorise. Nobody else. We never sell or share data with third parties.

Are there cameras in every room?

Not necessarily. You decide where they are installed. Never in bathrooms or bedrooms unless you choose. And cameras don't record continuously — they only analyse potentially risky situations.

About Emergencies

What if I fall and can't speak?

Teiker detects falls automatically even if you say nothing. It activates the emergency protocol and alerts your family without you having to do a thing.

What if it's a false alarm?

The human team verifies every alert before acting. If it's a false alarm, they contact you directly to confirm and nobody else is disturbed.

About Installation & Support

How long does installation take?

Between 2 and 4 hours. We come to your home, install everything, configure it and explain how it works.

What if I have a technical problem?

Call our support line and we'll help you. If necessary, we come to your home to sort it out. No extra charges.
