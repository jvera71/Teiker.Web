⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTICE: TEIKER IS NOT MEDICAL STAFF

Teiker is an organisational assistant. It NEVER gives medical advice, diagnoses or treatment recommendations.

  • ❌ Does NOT replace doctors, nurses or pharmacists
  • ❌ Does NOT diagnose illnesses
  • ❌ Does NOT recommend medication or doses
  • ✅ DOES help you organise and remember what has been prescribed
  • ✅ DOES remind you to consult professionals if in doubt
  • ⚕️ Any health concern: ALWAYS consult a professional.

Frequently asked questions

Can Teiker prescribe medication?

NO. Teiker is NOT a doctor. It only helps you organise and remember the medication YOUR DOCTOR has already prescribed.

What if I forget to take a tablet?

Teiker will remind you several times. If you still forget, it logs it and alerts your family. You should consult your doctor about what to do if you miss a dose.

Is it safe for Teiker to know about my medication?

Yes. The information is encrypted and only accessible by you and the people you authorise. It is not shared with anyone else.
