Automatic protection that acts for you

Teiker is not a button you have to press. It's a system that monitors your wellbeing constantly and acts automatically if it detects an emergency. You don't have to do anything. If you fall, if you feel dizzy, if you're in danger, Teiker detects it and activates a response protocol.

1

⚡ AUTOMATIC DETECTION

Time: Seconds 0–5

Teiker monitors your wellbeing continuously. If it detects a fall, a cry for help, a worrying pattern or any emergency situation, the alert is activated automatically.

What it detects:

Falls

Sudden falls, heavy impacts, abrupt immobility

Cries for Help

"Help!", "Call an ambulance!", screams of pain or distress

Medical Situations

Difficulty breathing, intense pain, extreme confusion

The key point:

✅ You don't have to do anything

✅ No devices to carry

✅ Works even when unconscious

✅ Acts even if you can't speak

2

👨‍💼 PROFESSIONAL VERIFICATION

Time: Seconds 5–30

When an alert is activated, a professional team at our monitoring centre reviews the situation in real time. Not automated — real people verifying what's happening.

Verification Process

Step 1: Context Review (Seconds 5–10)

What type of alert? Where did it happen? Any prior context?

Step 2: Contact Attempt (Seconds 10–25)

"Hello, we've detected a possible fall. Are you all right? Do you need help?"

Possible Outcomes:

False Alarm

You confirm you're fine. Alert cancelled. Nobody else is disturbed.

Minor Assistance

You need help getting up. Family is alerted, NOT the emergency services.

Real Emergency

No response or struggling to speak. Full protocol activated.

3

📞 IMMEDIATE CONTACT

Time: Seconds 25–60

Once the emergency is confirmed, the contact protocol is triggered. Your family receives an immediate call with all the information. And if necessary, the emergency services are notified at the same time.

Contact Protocol

1. Primary Contact

Phone call + SMS + App notification

2. Secondary Contact

If primary doesn't respond (40–50s)

Emergency Services 999/112

Simultaneous if serious (second 30)

What your family receives:

📱 Phone Call

Clear information, current status, actions taken

📨 SMS Backup

Summary, exact address, contact number

📲 App Notification

Push alert, location on map, further details

4

👁️ FOLLOW-UP UNTIL RESOLVED

Until everything is sorted

We don't leave your side until everything is resolved. The monitoring team stays in contact with you, your family and the emergency services until they confirm you are receiving the care you need.

Frequently asked questions about emergencies

How does Teiker know I've fallen?

Teiker monitors your wellbeing continuously. If it detects a sudden fall, abrupt immobility or hears words asking for help, it automatically activates the protocol. You don't need to wear anything special.

What if I fall but I'm fine? Will my family be frightened needlessly?

No. That's why there's human verification. When a fall is detected, an operator contacts you first. If you say you're fine, the alert is cancelled and nobody else is disturbed. The family is only notified if there really is an emergency.

Does it work at night?

Yes, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Emergencies don't keep office hours and neither does Teiker.

How long from when I fall to when my family is called?

Normally between 20 and 40 seconds. Detection (5s) + Verification (15–30s) + Call (immediate).
