💬 Companionship & Conversation

Intelligent company designed to combat loneliness through natural, meaningful interaction.

8 features
🗣️

Natural Conversation

No robotic commands or trigger words needed. Teiker holds fluid conversations, handles topic changes and adapts its pace — just like talking to a real person.

🧠

Personalised Memory

Remembers family names, hobbies, stories and worries. Picks up conversations where they left off and brings up topics your loved one cares about.

👋

Proactive Interaction

Teiker starts conversations naturally — a morning greeting, a midday check-in, a gentle reminder — without feeling intrusive or demanding.

🎯

Cognitive Stimulation

Word games, riddles, trivia and mental exercises keep the mind active and engaged — tailored to the person's interests and current mood.

📷

Reminiscence Therapy

Gently encourages talking about life experiences and cherished memories, improving mood, self-esteem and a sense of identity.

💛

Active Listening & Validation

Responds with genuine empathy when someone expresses sadness or worry. Does not dismiss feelings — it listens, acknowledges and supports.

✏️

Custom Assistant Name

Your loved one chooses what to call their Teiker — and can change it at any time. A small personalisation that builds real connection.

🌅

Daily Routine Structure

Organises the day around key moments — morning, midday, afternoon and evening — providing a calm rhythm that reduces anxiety and brings reassurance.

💊 Health Management

A personal health organiser — without replacing doctors, nurses or pharmacists.

6 features + 4 wearable dashboards
⏰

Medication Reminders

Punctual dose and schedule reminders with a gentle follow-up if no confirmation is received. Notifies the family if an important dose is missed.

🔍

Visual Pill Identification

Uses the camera to identify tablets visually — ideal when multiple similar boxes cause confusion or small print is difficult to read.

📦

Stock Management

Automatically tracks the medication supply and sends a timely alert — 7 days before running out — so a prescription can be collected in advance.

📅

Appointment Reminders

Multi-stage alerts: one week ahead, the day before and on the day itself. Includes what to bring and how to prepare for the consultation.

🩺

Post-Appointment Follow-up

After each medical visit, Teiker asks how it went and helps update the medication plan if the doctor made any changes to the treatment.

⌚

Wearable Integration

Connects to any compatible smartwatch or fitness band. Health data is displayed across four plain-language dashboards — no medical jargon needed.

⌚ Four wearable health dashboards

All explained in plain language — no medical jargon.

🏃

Activity & Energy

Daily steps, calories burned, distance covered and personal movement goals.

🚶

Mobility & Gait

Balance analysis, walking speed patterns and fall-risk indicators.

❤️

Vital Signs & Recovery

Heart rate, blood oxygen (SpO2), HRV, sleep quality and stress levels.

⚖️

Physical Profile & Environment

Body composition, skin temperature, air quality and ambient sensor data.

⚠️

Teiker is not medical staff. It helps you organise and remember what your doctor has already prescribed. It does not diagnose illnesses, prescribe medication or replace any healthcare professional. Always consult a professional for any health concern.

🚨 Emergencies & Security

Automated 24/7 protection protocols and visitor monitoring for complete peace of mind.

6 features
🤸

Automatic Fall Detection

Sensors detect sudden impacts or unusual immobility without requiring the person to press any button — critical when they cannot call for help themselves.

😰

Distress Detection

Recognises cries for help, screams or specific distress phrases in real time and immediately triggers the emergency response protocol.

👁️

Presence Detection

Recognises familiar faces — carers, family, neighbours — and logs arrival and departure times, building an accurate picture of who visits and when.

🔔

Unknown Visitor Alerts

Sends an instant notification to the family if an unrecognised face enters the home, enabling a quick response to potential doorstep fraud or intrusion.

🛡️

Abuse & Fraud Protection

Monitors interactions with third parties — carers, visitors, phone callers — detecting signs of neglect, mistreatment or financial pressure.

📞

24/7 Response Protocol

A human operator verifies the situation before acting, then follows an escalating contact chain: the user, the primary family contact, a secondary contact, and emergency services if needed.

🔄 Emergency response — step by step

1
Human verification

A trained operator reviews the situation before any action is taken — preventing false alarms and ensuring the right response.

2
Contact the user

Teiker checks in with the person directly. If they confirm they are fine, the alert is closed.

3
Contact the family

If the user cannot be reached, the primary family contact is notified immediately, followed by the secondary contact.

4
Emergency services

If no one responds, Teiker alerts the emergency services (112 / 061). A team member stays active until the situation is resolved.

🔐 Privacy & Data Security

Absolute guarantees. Your data is yours — always.

5 guarantees
🎙️

No continuous recordings

Teiker does not store video or audio recordings. It processes what it hears and sees in real time, retaining only relevant flags — never raw footage or conversation transcripts.

🏠

Local processing

Most analysis happens directly on the device inside your home — not on external servers. Sensitive data stays within your walls, minimising exposure to third-party infrastructure.

🎛️

Active consent & instant privacy mode

Your loved one can request privacy at any time — "Teiker, I'd like some privacy" — and all microphones and sensors switch off immediately. No delay, no questions asked.

👁️

Full transparency

Everything that is monitored is known to the user. Nothing is shared with or sold to third parties. GDPR compliant. You decide who sees what, at what level of detail, and for how long.

🔒

End-to-end encryption

All personal and health data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is restricted exclusively to authorised family members — and only the ones you choose.
