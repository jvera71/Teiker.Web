Conversations that matter

Teiker converses the way a good companion would

🗣️

Talk the way you'd talk to anyone

No special words, no exact commands, no robotic phrasing needed. Teiker understands you the way your family does. Change topics, ask questions, tell stories… just like any real conversation.

Example: Natural start

You: "Teiker, what a lovely day it is today"

Teiker: "Wonderful, isn't it? Great weather for a walk. Any plans to get out today?"

You: "Not sure, my knee's playing up a bit"

Teiker: "Oh, I'm sorry to hear that. Is it the same knee from the other day when you went shopping? Has it got better or is it still bothering you?"

Example: Natural topic changes

You: "I was thinking about my mother today"

Teiker: "Was there something in particular that reminded you?"

You: "I saw some flowers she loved. Sweet peas."

Teiker: "What a lovely memory. You've mentioned her before, haven't you? Tell me more if you'd like."

What people ask us about Teiker conversations

About Conversation

Does Teiker only respond when I speak, or can it start conversations?

Teiker can do both. It responds when you speak, but it can also start conversations at appropriate moments (good morning, checking how you are, appointment reminders). Always naturally and never intrusively.

Can I talk about any topic?

Yes. Teiker is prepared to talk about anything: news, family, memories, hobbies, culture, current affairs, health… whatever you want. And if it doesn't know something, it says so and tries to help you find it.

Can I tell it to stop if I'm not in the mood?

Of course. Just say "I don't want to chat right now" or "Let's talk later" and Teiker will respect that. It's never pushy or intrusive.

About Personalisation

How does Teiker learn my tastes and my story?

It gets to know you through conversation. At first it will ask questions to understand you better. After that, it remembers naturally everything you share.
